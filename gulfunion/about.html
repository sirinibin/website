Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

2010

Foundation

Gulf Union Ozone was founded with a vision to become the UAE's premier industrial supplies specialist, starting with a small warehouse in Dubai's Al Quoz Industrial Area.

2013

Expansion

Expanded operations to serve major construction and mining companies, establishing partnerships with leading international filter manufacturers.

2018

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, demonstrating our commitment to quality management and customer satisfaction.

2022

Digital Innovation

Launched our advanced online catalog and quotation system, making it easier for clients to find and order the right filters for their equipment.

2025

Today

Now serving over 50,000 industrial clients across the Kingdom with a comprehensive inventory of over 50,000 filter products from world-renowned manufacturers.
