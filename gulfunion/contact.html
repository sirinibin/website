Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What are your delivery times?

Same-day delivery in Riyadh for orders placed before 2 PM. Next-day delivery to major cities (Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar). 2-3 days for other locations within Saudi Arabia.

Do you provide technical consultation?

Yes, our certified filtration specialists provide free technical consultation to help you select the right filters for your equipment. We also offer on-site visits for complex requirements.

What warranty do you offer on filters?

All our filters come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the product. We also provide our own quality guarantee for defect-free performance.

Can you source hard-to-find filters?

Absolutely! With our global network of suppliers and manufacturers, we can source specialized filters for older equipment or unique applications. Contact us with your specifications.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer competitive volume discounts for bulk orders and long-term contracts. Contact our sales team for customized pricing based on your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, company cheques, and credit terms for established corporate customers. Cash payments are accepted for retail purchases at our facility.

Emergency Filter Requirements?

Equipment breakdown? Critical maintenance? We provide 24/7 emergency support for urgent filter requirements to minimize your downtime.
